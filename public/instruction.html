Trivia Game

Instructions

Welcome to the Trivia game! We hope you enjoy!

To begin, simply click the start button below. Beware though, it is timed, be ready to start answering questions the moment you click start. Once the game has started, randomly generated trivia questions will appear on the screen. Answer the question and the next one will appear. Good Luck and have fun!
